Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 March 2026

Pinnacle Apps ("we", "us", or "our") operates the COSMIQ mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use COSMIQ.

By using COSMIQ, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account and use the App:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name
  • Birth data: Your date of birth, time of birth, and place of birth — used to calculate your astrological birth chart
  • Journal entries: Any text you write in the COSMIQ journal feature
  • Compatibility data: Birth details of any people you add for compatibility readings
  • Usage data: General information about how you interact with the App, used to improve the experience

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate your personalised birth chart and daily horoscopes
  • Sync your journal entries and profile across your devices
  • Process your subscription and manage your account
  • Send you daily horoscope notifications (if enabled)
  • Improve and maintain the App
  • Respond to your support requests

3. Third-Party Services

COSMIQ uses the following third-party services to operate:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication. Your account data and journal entries are stored securely on Supabase servers. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic — AI-generated horoscope content. Your astrological sign data (not your name or email) is sent to Anthropic's API to generate personalised horoscope text. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management. Purchase and subscription status is managed via RevenueCat. See RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store — All payments are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment details.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by Supabase. We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorised access, disclosure, or loss. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at velastudio.app@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

COSMIQ is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
